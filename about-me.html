Jignesh Patel

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Vadodara (IIITV)- International Campus Diu (ICD). My research interests are Deep learning, Computer vision and Remote Sensing. My current work focuses on Communication, Hyperspectral imaging, using Machine learning.

I received my PhD in 2022 under the guidance of Prof. Manjunath V. Joshi at DA-IICT, Gandhinagar, India. During my time at DA-IICT, I also served as a Teaching Assistant for various undergraduate, graduate, and Ph.D. courses. Prior to that, I obtained my M.E. in 2012 from GTU, Ahmedabad, India, and my B.E. in 2009 from HNGU, Patan.
